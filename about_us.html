Tiny Tots, a company founded in 2020 by Emilia Galach, is dedicated to providing parents with comprehensive childcare knowledge and support. With a focus on infants and toddlers, Tiny Tots offers a range of courses and groups that equip parents with practical skills to enhance their children's well-being and promote healthy development.

Tiny Tots is a valuable resource for parents seeking to enhance their childcare knowledge and skills. Through its comprehensive courses and groups, the company provides a supportive environment where parents can learn from experts, connect with others, and nurture the well-being of their infants and toddlers. By empowering parents with essential childcare techniques, Tiny Tots contributes to the healthy development and happiness of young children and their families.
