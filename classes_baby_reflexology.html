Baby reflexology is a specialized technique that harnesses the power of gentle pressure on specific reflex points located on a baby's tiny feet to stimulate and support the harmonious functioning of their developing organs and systems. This ancient practice, adapted for infants, works on the principle that these reflex points correspond to specific areas within the body, creating a natural pathway for energy flow and promoting overall well-being.

Tiny Tots, a leading provider of baby reflexology classes, offers professional trainers and facilitators who guide parents through the techniques and principles of effective baby reflexology.

Benefits of baby reflexology

By applying gentle, rhythmic strokes and pressures to these points, awide range of common infant discomforts, such as colic, gas, constipation, and sleep disturbances can be address.

The gentle stimulation of these reflex points can:

  • aid in digestion, promote relaxation
  • encourage a sense of calm fostering a more peaceful and contented baby
  • be a valuable complementary therapy, offering a safe and effective way to support a baby's health and development
